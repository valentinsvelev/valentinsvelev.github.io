About Me

Hello and welcome to my webpage!

My name is Valentin Velev and I am a data science student at the University of Konstanz with experience in deep learning (e.g., NLP), machine learning (e.g., regression for prediction and classification, PCA, topic modeling, clustering), data analysis (e.g., EDA, causality, econometrics), and academic research. I currently work as a data science working student at Codify AG and a student assistant at the Department of Computer and Information Science at the University of Konstanz. My research interest lies in utilizing deep learning models, primarily natural language processing (NLP) models, to solve complex problems across various academic and industry domains.
